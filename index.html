Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help remember preferences and improve your experience.

2. Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies — expire when you close your browser
  • Preference cookies — remember settings like selected currency

3. Analytics Cookies

  • _ga — Google Analytics, 2 years
  • _gid — Google Analytics, 24 hours

4. Managing Cookies

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

5. Consent

By continuing to use our website you consent to our use of cookies. Withdraw consent at any time via browser settings.
